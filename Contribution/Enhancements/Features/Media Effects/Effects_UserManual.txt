Media Effects — User Manual (CDN Version)

Feature
Adds cinematic visual effects (Glitch, VHS, BlurPulse) to images or videos on a slide.

How to Use
1) Open this HTML file in a browser. It works anywhere since it loads Reveal.js from CDN.
2) On the Media Effects slide, click one of the effect buttons (Glitch, VHS, BlurPulse).
3) The effect is applied instantly to all elements with class="fx-target".
4) Click "None" to remove effects.

Advantages
• Enhances visuals with attention-grabbing styles.
• Fully CSS-based — smooth and lightweight.
• CDN links ensure it works from any folder.
