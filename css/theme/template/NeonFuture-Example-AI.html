AI refers to the simulation of human intelligence in machines that are programmed to think, learn, and solve problems.

General AI

Hypothetical AI with human-like cognitive abilities

History of AI

  • 1950s

    Alan Turing proposes the Turing Test

  • 1956

    Term "Artificial Intelligence" coined at Dartmouth Conference

  • 1997

    IBM's Deep Blue defeats chess champion Garry Kasparov

  • 2011

    IBM Watson wins Jeopardy! against champions

  • 2016

    AlphaGo defeats world champion in Go

Key AI Technologies

Machine Learning

Systems that learn from data without explicit programming

Neural Networks

Computing systems inspired by the human brain

Natural Language Processing

Enabling machines to understand and process human language

Computer Vision

Machines that can interpret and understand visual information
